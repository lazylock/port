Apparel

Fall 2014 - Spring 2015

Throughout high school I designed t-shirts for campus organizations, and I eventually founded the Digital Art & Design Club to build a community of artists and designers. There was a pressing need for graphic work, and the usual route for clubs was to crowdsource a design, a process that was lengthy and did not guarantee results. We educated students interested in the field through workshops and gave them real projects from other clubs. Beyond this leadership role, I completed the shirts below. All of the art was done in Adobe Illustrator. This was the first time I'd actually see any of my work, not only in physical form, but also being used by others.
