Spring 2018

We brought the Drip Drop team together along with some new faces for our spring film. With a picture of a soapy waffle and the line "If you love a flower, don't pick it", we created a story centered around geocaching and friendship. The sheer variety and number of locations made the shoot ambitious, but we were able to do a full circle around the southern half of the Bay in the course of a single day. I'm especially proud of the level of production value we achieved in highlighting this area's beauty.
