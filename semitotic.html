Spring 2018

My friend Jack Virnich recruited me as cinematographer for his project for an experimental film class. This was a compressed version of a feature-length script he wrote in the winter. The result takes us on a journey through a surreal world in which people embody signs and also examines whether we are truly bound to fate.
